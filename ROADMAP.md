## Simple Deployment Strategy

### Hosting (Free/Low Cost)
- **Vercel** or **Netlify** - Free tier with custom domain
- **GitHub Pages** - Alternative free option
- **No backend needed** - Everything runs client-side with localStorage

### Monitoring & Analytics
- **Google Analytics** - Free usage tracking
- **Sentry** - Free error monitoring (5k errors/month)
- **Vercel Analytics** - Free if using Vercel

### Estimated Costs
- **Domain**: ~$10-15/year (optional)
- **Premium hosting**: $0-20/month (if outgrowing free tiers)
- **Total**: $0-35/month maximum

## Risk Mitigation (Solo Context)

### Technical Risks
- **Over-engineering** - Keep it simple, avoid complex solutions
- **Technical debt** - Maintain test coverage, regular refactoring
- **Dependency issues** - Pin versions, regular updates

### Personal Risks
- **Burnout** - Maintain sustainable pace, celebrate small wins
- **Scope creep** - Focus on core value, resist feature bloat
- **Perfectionism** - Ship early, iterate based on real usage

### Business Risks
- **Low usage** - Focus on personal use case, share with friends/family
- **Maintenance burden** - Choose stable, well-supported technologies
- **Feature complexity** - Add features only when clearly needed
